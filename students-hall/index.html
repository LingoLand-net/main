Chahd M.

Grade 10 Student

I'm Bessem Mbarek's favorite student and quite frankly who's surprised? But this post isn't just about me it's about Lingoland: the book that made all the difference. If you haven't heard of LingoLand, you're seriously missing out. It's not just an English textbook it's a full experience. Designed by the one and only Mr. Bessem himself. LingoLand is smart, fun, and made for us. Real topics, real conversations, and exercises that actually help you use the language not just memorize it from the first page, I could tell it was different. The lessons felt like they were made for me and the way everything's explained just clicks. What really made a difference? The interactive listening and speaking exercises gave me that extra confidence to speak like a native. The grammar and vocabulary weren't just "rules" but practical things I could actually use every day. And the best part? It never felt like work. It felt like I was just having a conversation or learning something cool with every chapter. So yeah, I've always been good at English, but Lingoland made me even better.
